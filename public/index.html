our engagements

Fixed fee · One week

Project-based · Two to eight weeks

About

Where protocol design
meets trading reality

Most technical advisors come from a single discipline — either security auditing or production systems engineering. Cambie operates at the intersection: protocol-level correctness, adversarial security thinking, and real trading infrastructure experience.

This overlap — programmable financial contracts, market-making systems, risk engines, and execution infrastructure — is where the most expensive problems live, and where generalist agencies and traditional auditors both fall short.

We keep our client roster deliberately small. Each engagement informs the next, compounding into a proprietary understanding of how financial systems break, scale, and perform.
